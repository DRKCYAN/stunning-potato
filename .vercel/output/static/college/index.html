College Applications by Immigration Status

Your visa status can determine whether you pay in-state or out-of-state tuition, whether you can apply to public universities, and what state financial aid you can access. These rules vary dramatically by state — a student who qualifies for in-state tuition in California may not in Texas, and vice versa.
