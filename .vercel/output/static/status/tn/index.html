TN Professional (USMCA)

TN status is available to Canadian and Mexican citizens who qualify to work in one of the designated professional occupations listed in the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA, formerly NAFTA). TN holders must work for the specific petitioning employer in the specific profession listed on their approval. Canadian citizens may apply at the border or a U.S. port of entry; Mexican citizens must apply for a TN visa at a U.S. consulate.
