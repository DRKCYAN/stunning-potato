H-4 Dependent

H-4 status is available to the spouse and unmarried children under age 21 of H-1B principal visa holders. H-4 status itself does not authorize employment. However, qualifying H-4 spouses may apply for an Employment Authorization Document (EAD) if the H-1B principal has an approved Form I-140 immigrant petition or has been granted an extension of H-1B status beyond the 6-year cap limit under the American Competitiveness in the Twenty-first Century Act (AC21). H-4 children are not eligible for work authorization.
