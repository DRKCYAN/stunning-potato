What this site is — and isn't

KnowYourStatus is a free, open-source reference. Every answer links to a primary source — a federal statute, regulation, or official agency page — so you can read the law yourself. This site does not provide legal advice, does not use AI-generated content, and carries no ads. For guidance on your specific situation, please consult a licensed attorney or accredited representative. Learn more →

Legal information, not legal advice

This page provides general legal information for educational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice and does not create an attorney-client relationship. Immigration law is complex and fact-specific. For advice about your individual situation, consult a licensed immigration attorney or DOJ-accredited representative.
